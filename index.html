My name is William Jack Arnesen. I am a policy manager at the Market Shaping Accelerator at the University of Chicago (2023-). I hold a Bachelor's degree from Yale University in Economics & Mathematics (2020). I previously worked on markets at Kalshi (2021-2023), and as a research analyst at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York (2020-21). I will be starting a Ph.D. in Business Economics at Harvard University beginning in fall 2025.

I am principally interested in the economics of innovation and industrial organization. My research at the University of Chicago has focused on estimating the appropriate size of advance market commitments for novel innovations in public goods.
